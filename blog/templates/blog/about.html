About Me

Currently working at world's leading EDA tool company, the job is more related to semiconductor manufacturing. I had my bachelor degree in telecommunication engineering and my master degree in System-on-chip design.

I am enthusiastic about technologies. I am willing to learn new techniques and willing to turn them into practice. I love Python, a lot: building web sites, automation with scripts, data analysis and processing, computer vision - all of these are making me excited.

In the free time, travelling would be the most pleasant thing I would like to spend with my family. Taking photographs along the journey and making them an album with stories is really comforting.

I keep the habit of writing articles in English, for the reason that I like to keep knowledge of technology recorded in the international language so that it could be shared by more people and be valuable to more people. Besides, I need to practise it anyway.
